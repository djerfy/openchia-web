From now on OpenChia will no longer be doing weekly giveaways.

We have decided to stop running weekly giveaways for a number of reasons.

First of all, we have noticed little to no engagement from our community in regards to that. We often found ourselves doing the livestream to draw the winner number and we did not have anybody watching and the number of views afterwards were also very slim.

A poll was made in our Discord and Twitter regarding the giveaway versus discount fees for all farmers. It turns out people preferred discount fees and that is what we implemented and pushed live before ending the giveaway.

Finally, the giveaway is no longer creating the interest and return that we were hoping for and given poolingâ€™s thin margins it makes little sense to continue increasing debt unnecessarily.

We hope you all understand the decision.
