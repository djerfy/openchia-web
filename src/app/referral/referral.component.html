Referral
BETA

Earn XCH by referring your friends to this pool!

How does it work?

  • Refer your friends to this pool using the URL https://openchia.io?referrer=YOUR_LAUNCHER_ID. Replace YOUR_LAUNCHER_ID with the Launcher ID from your Plot NFT.
  • Have your friend join the pool and login to the pool website using the Plot NFT login link. He will confirm you are his referrer. (Referrer is filled automatically if he visited the website with your referral URL)
  • You will receive 20% of the pool fee for every payment made to your friend. That value comes out of the pool and not from your friend reward!

How do I check my referrals?

Login to the pool website using your Plot NFT login link and go to the "Referrals" tab.

Rules

  • Use of good faith and don't refer yourself in any shape or form.
  • Do not attempt close loop of referral. e.g. A -> B -> C -> A.
  • We reserve the right to change fees for new referrals at any time.
